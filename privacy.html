Privacy Policy

Last updated: October

1. Information We Collect

Smart-Wiki collects basic account information such as your name, email address, and profile photo when you sign in using Google authentication. We also store content you create within the application, including documents and metadata.

2. Google Calendar Data

If you explicitly choose to connect your Google Calendar, Smart-Wiki may access read-only calendar data to display upcoming events inside the application. We do not modify, delete, or share your calendar data.

3. Use of Information

Your data is used solely to provide core functionality such as authentication, document management, collaboration, and optional calendar integration. We do not sell or rent user data.

4. Google API Services

Smart-Wiki’s use of information received from Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including access controls and encrypted connections.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data.
